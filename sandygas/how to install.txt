How To Install:

1.Open your folder where it contains all of your server files, documents etc
2.Go to Resources and drag and drop the ymap folder into that resources folder.
3.Last, go to your server.cfg and add start ymap

NOW GO ENJOY YOUR NEW YMAP!

NOTE: FROM NOW ON YOU CAN JUST ADD ANY YMAPS INTO YOUR YMAP-STREAM FOLDER AND THEN
RESTART YOUR SERVER AND ENJOY THEM!
